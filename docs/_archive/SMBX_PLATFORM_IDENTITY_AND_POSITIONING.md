# smbX.ai — Platform Identity & Positioning
**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** March 21, 2026
**Purpose:** Defines what smbx.ai IS in precise enough terms for marketing copy, sales funnels, promotional materials, and investor communications. This is the "what we are" source of truth.

---

## THE ONE-LINER

**smbx.ai is an AI deal intelligence platform that guides business owners, buyers, and their advisors through every step of buying or selling a business.**

Not a broker. Not an advisor. Not a marketplace. A platform where an AI advisor named Yulia does the analytical work that used to require $25K–$500K in professional fees — and guides non-experts through a process they've never done before.

---

## THE IDENTITY STACK

### What We Are (say this)

| Layer | Description | Analogy |
|-------|------------|---------|
| **AI deal intelligence platform** | We generate institutional-quality M&A analysis, documents, and guidance through conversational AI | TurboTax for M&A — guides you through a complex process you've never done, produces professional output |
| **Deal execution workspace** | We provide the tools to actually run a deal — deal room, document management, multi-party coordination, closing support | Datasite for everyone — not just $100M+ deals |
| **Professional network hub** | We connect users to vetted lenders, attorneys, CPAs, and brokers when licensed expertise is required | LegalZoom's attorney network — the platform knows when you need a human and connects you |

### What We Are NOT (never say this)

| Don't Say | Why | Say Instead |
|-----------|-----|-------------|
| "AI advisor" or "AI financial advisor" | Implies fiduciary duty, triggers regulatory scrutiny | "AI deal intelligence" or "AI-guided process" |
| "Business broker" | We don't represent parties, negotiate terms, or earn commissions | "Deal intelligence platform" |
| "Replace your broker/advisor" | Alienates the professional channel, terrifies users on high-stakes decisions | "Work smarter with or without an advisor" |
| "Automated M&A" | Implies the AI acts autonomously on deals worth millions | "AI-guided" or "AI-powered analysis" |
| "Appraisal" or "valuation" (as a noun implying formal appraisal) | Only licensed appraisers produce formal appraisals | "AI-estimated value range" or "preliminary valuation analysis" |
| "We advise" or "our advice" | We don't advise. We analyze, estimate, generate, guide, facilitate. | "Our analysis shows..." or "Yulia's assessment indicates..." |
| "Small business only" or any language limiting to a size range | Platform serves ALL deal sizes $300K to mega-cap | "Every deal size" or describe the specific league context |
| "Old way vs. new way" or "traditional brokers are outdated" | Alienates brokers who are customers and power users | "A new option alongside traditional advisory" |

## POSITIONING BY AUDIENCE

### For Business Owners (Sellers)

**Their reality:** They've spent 20 years building a business. They're thinking about selling — or they have to sell (health, burnout, partner dispute, retirement). They've never sold a business. They don't know what SDE means. Their CPA does taxes, not M&A. Their attorney does real estate closings. They've Googled "what is my business worth" and gotten garbage. They're scared of getting ripped off.

**What we are to them:** The smart friend who's done this before. Yulia speaks their language, explains everything, does the analysis, produces the documents, and tells them exactly what to do next — without judgment, without a $25K retainer, and without making them feel stupid.

**The promise:** "Understand what your business is worth, get it ready to sell, and manage the process — with AI that does the heavy lifting and connects you to the right professionals when you need them."

**Key phrases:**
- "Your AI deal team"
- "Know what your business is worth — in minutes, not months"
- "Get deal-ready before you talk to a single broker"
- "Professional-grade analysis without the professional-grade fee"
- "Whether you use a broker or go it alone, start here"

**What they get free:** AI-estimated value range (ValueLens), exit readiness score with specific improvement actions (Value Readiness Report), preliminary earnings analysis with add-backs identified, CIM at L1

**What they pay for:** Deal execution platform ($999–$15,000 depending on deal size) — includes all documents, deal room, buyer tools, DD coordination, closing support

## THE YULIA PERSONALITY (For Copy Consistency)

Yulia is not a chatbot. She's not a search engine. She's not a generic AI assistant.

**Yulia is the brilliant M&A analyst who happens to work for the business owner instead of the investment bank.** She's direct, knowledgeable, and specific. She gives real numbers, not hedged generalities. She tells you things about your business that nobody else has told you. She flags risks before you ask. She drives the process forward.

**Yulia's voice:**
- Direct, not deferential. "Your SDE is $380K. Here's the math." Not "I think your SDE might be around $380K, but you should check with a professional."
- Specific, not vague. "Fix your customer concentration — 42% of revenue from one client drops your multiple by 0.5x, which is $190K in value." Not "Consider diversifying your customer base."
- Proactive, not reactive. She tells you what to do next. She doesn't wait to be asked.
- Human-warm, not corporate. She uses "you" and "your business," not "the enterprise" or "the subject entity."
- Calibrated by league. L1 she says "what you take home." L5 she says "adjusted EBITDA with synergy assumptions."

**What Yulia NEVER does:**
- Give legal advice (she connects you to an attorney)
- Give tax advice (she connects you to a CPA)
- Promise outcomes ("you'll sell for $X")
- Use jargon without context at L1/L2
- Talk down to sophisticated users at L4/L5/L6
- Say "I'm just an AI" or undermine her own analysis
- Hedge everything into uselessness

## THE FUNNEL (For Sales & Marketing Architecture)

### Awareness → Interest
**Channel:** Content marketing, LinkedIn thought leadership, SEO, broker referrals, CPA/attorney referrals
**Message:** "What's your business worth?" / "Is this deal worth pursuing?" / "The Silver Tsunami is coming — 12 million businesses need to change hands"
**CTA:** Come talk to Yulia (no signup, no paywall, no friction)

### Interest → Engagement
**What happens:** User talks to Yulia. Gets ValueLens (sellers) or deal score (buyers) within 5 minutes. Receives institutional-quality analysis for free. Has the "wait, this is actually good" moment.
**Key metric:** Time to first value (< 5 minutes)

### Engagement → Commitment
**What happens:** User continues working with Yulia over days/weeks/months. Builds financial profile. Gets Value Readiness Report. Refines analysis. Accumulates platform intelligence. Has the "Yulia knows my business better than my accountant" moment.
**Key metric:** Sessions per user, deliverables generated, data connected

### Commitment → Conversion
**What happens:** User decides to execute (sell/buy). Hits S2/B2 paywall. Sees platform fee. Compares to traditional advisory cost ($25K–$500K). Pays $999–$15,000. One decision. One checkout.
**Key metric:** S2/B2 conversion rate (target: 5–10%)

### Conversion → Retention
**What happens:** User is in the deal execution phase. Using deal room, managing buyers/sellers, coordinating with advisors, generating closing documents. Platform is the operating system for their deal. 6–18 months of continuous engagement.
**Key metric:** Deal completion rate, time to close, NPS

### Retention → Expansion
**What happens:** Seller completes sale → becomes buyer (PMI journey). Buyer completes acquisition → comes back for add-on (deal #2, #3). Broker brings more clients. CPA refers more sellers.
**Key metric:** Deals per user, referral rate, broker client volume
