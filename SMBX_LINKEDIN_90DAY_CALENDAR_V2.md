## Part 1 — Voice Rules (read these once before writing anything)

**Do:**
- Lead with the result. Days. Dollars. Counts. Percentages.
- Short lines. Single sentences become single lines.
- Specific numbers everywhere. Vague kills.
- The deliverable is the proof. The result is the hero.
- Branded terms (Baseline, Blind Equity, Rundown) used sparingly. Open loops, not the message.
- Disclaimers tight, italicized, end of post. One line max.
- Founder POV posts close with the offer.

**Don't:**
- No replacement language. Never frame this as a human getting cut.
  - Banned: "the analyst pod you don't have," "the second junior you didn't hire," "do you really need them."
- No "btw this solves" — still throat-clearing.
- No persona pain references in the body. The result speaks to the persona.
- No corporate voice. No "leverage," "elevate," "seamless."
- No long setup paragraphs.
- No exclamation marks.

**Frame for every post:** what got done, in how long, with what proof.
