Four add-backs every owner misses.

01
Above-market rent to your own LLC
If you own the real estate and charge yourself 20% above market, that difference is normalizable — and usually worth $40K–$80K per year.
02
Owner comp above replacement
A buyer will pay a general manager market wage. Anything you pay yourself beyond that is an add-back.
03
One-time legal and professional
Litigation, one-off acquisitions, systems implementations. Non-recurring is a legitimate normalization.
04
Personal expenses on the books
Family auto, phone, travel, meals. Documented, defensible, and typically 3–5% of revenue in owner-operated businesses.
