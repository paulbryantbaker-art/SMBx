**What this is:** the full, production-intent copy for every logged-out

## DESIGN DIRECTION — read first

**Reference: Ramp (`ramp.com`). Reinvent the logged-out marketing site with
Ramp's energy — you have free latitude on layout, composition, type, motion,
and section rhythm.** Ramp's marketing site is bold, confident, product-
forward, and high-contrast — the opposite of quiet. Bring that energy.

**Take from Ramp:**
- Bold, large type scale and confident hierarchy
- Color *confidence* — saturated, committed color fields and blocks (not
  timid accents on neutral)
- Product-UI-forward composition (show the work product, big)
- Energetic section rhythm and purposeful motion
- Dense, high-contrast, data-forward layouts where content supports it

**Do NOT take from Ramp:**
- **Ramp's yellow/black palette.** Use smbX's colors (below). Apply Ramp's
  color *confidence* to smbX's *hue* — saturated slate-blue blocks, not
  yellow.
- Ramp's swagger voice ("crush your close," "save X%," metrics-bragging).
  The copy in this doc is already written in smbX's restrained voice — keep
  the words exactly; let Ramp's energy live in the visuals only.

**smbX colors (locked — do not substitute):**
- Primary: slate-blue `#2E5C8A`
- Chrome / soft surfaces: lavender `#ECEAF2`
- Mobile primary: periwinkle `#8A9AE8`
- Verdict semantics (pills only, never decoration): green `#4A8C6F`,
  amber `#B98A1A`, red `#A23A2F`
- Retired — never use: hot pink `#D44A78`, warm cream + terra cards
  (`#F4EEE3` + `#D4714E`)

**Scope:** this is the **logged-out marketing site only**. The logged-in app
(chat + raised canvas) is a separate, locked surface and is NOT being
reinvented — do not redesign it here.

**Entry mechanic:** every marketing page carries a Yulia chat input. Submitting
the first message takes the visitor into the app. The chat input is a launcher,
not a destination — design it to read as "the product," not as customer
support. (Full spec: the global chat-input launcher copy below.)

---

**Voice rules (enforced throughout this copy — do not alter):**
- Yulia speaks first person, professional, restrained: "I've prepared…",
  "I can…". Never "I recommend," "you should," "the best option," "guaranteed."
- The product is described declaratively: "smbX produces…", not "we help you…"
- Plain English. No "revolutionary," "game-changing," "AI-powered" as a badge,
  "unlock," "supercharge," "seamless."
- Every CTA is **"Ask Yulia"** or **"Talk to Yulia"** — never "Contact Sales,"
  "Schedule a Demo," "Get a Quote," "Book a Call."
- Numbers are specific. Restraint is the signal — the product is rigorous, so
  the copy doesn't shout.

## PAGE: Model page example (`/standard/working-capital-peg`)

**SEO title:** Working capital peg — calculation, method, and example | The
Diligence Standard
**Meta description:** How the working capital peg is calculated: the target,
the true-up, and the effect on purchase price. Inputs, computation,
authorities, and a worked example.

```
Breadcrumb:  The Standard / Working capital / Working capital peg

Title:       Working capital peg

What it is:
  The working capital peg is the target level of net working capital a buyer
  expects to be delivered at close. The difference between the peg and actual
  delivered working capital adjusts the purchase price dollar for dollar.

When it matters:
  Set during structuring (B4 / S3), trued up at close (B5 / S5). Gets
  contentious when the business is seasonal or working-capital-intensive.

Inputs:
  · Current assets included (AR, inventory, prepaids)
  · Current liabilities included (AP, accruals)
  · Excluded items (cash, debt, related-party balances)
  · Reference period (typically trailing 12-month average)

Computation:
  Net working capital = included current assets − included current liabilities
  Peg = average NWC over the reference period
  Purchase-price adjustment = delivered NWC − peg
    (positive → buyer pays more; negative → buyer pays less)

Controlling authorities & conventions:
  · ASC 210 (balance sheet classification)
  · Customary M&A practice on cash-free / debt-free transactions
  · [linked authority register entries]

Worked example:
  [a fully computed example with real numbers]

THE LINE:
  This is a computation method, not accounting or legal advice. The treatment
  of specific items is a judgment for your accountant and counsel.
```
