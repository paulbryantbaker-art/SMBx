FAQ

The questions buyers ask before signing up.

Is there a success fee?
No. Banks take 3–6% of deal value. We take a monthly subscription. On a $10M deal you keep $300–600K that would otherwise go to the banker.
How long until I need to upgrade from Explore?
Most owners upgrade to Deal the week they decide to actually go to market — typically 4–12 weeks after first chat. Explore is designed to help you decide; Deal is designed to close.
Do I still need a lawyer?
Yes. Yulia drafts. Your counsel red-lines. Deals that skip legal review fall apart in diligence — we design Deal around that workflow, not in place of it.
What if the deal doesn't close?
You paid subscription for months of work, same as you'd have paid salary to a team. You keep every artifact — CIM, model, buyer list, diligence pack — for the next process. Unlike a banker's retainer, none of it burns on a re-trade.
