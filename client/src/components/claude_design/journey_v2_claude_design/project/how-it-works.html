The math

How we price, how we score, how we stress-test.

Three formulas power most of what Yulia outputs. They're not secret. If you want to argue with a number, argue with the math — and Yulia will show you her work.

Valuation range
Normalized EBITDA × multiple band · adjusted for quality score and macro position.
EV_low  = (EBITDA_reported + Σ addbacks_defensible) × mult_p25 × quality_adj
EV_high = (EBITDA_reported + Σ addbacks_defensible) × mult_p75 × quality_adj
quality_adj = 0.7 + 0.006 × readiness_score      // 70 = 1.12×
Readiness score
17 dimensions, each 0–10, weighted by what moves multiple in the sector.
score = Σ (dim_i × weight_i) × 100 / Σ weight_i
dims  = [rev_quality, margin, concentration, owner_dep,
         mgmt_depth, recurring, growth, working_cap, …]
Cap stack stress test
Fixed-charge coverage ratio across a 3×3 grid of EBITDA and rate shocks.
FCCV(Δ) = (EBITDA × (1 + Δ_ebitda) − capex) / (interest × (1 + Δ_rate) + principal)
breach  if FCCV(Δ) < covenant for any (Δ_ebitda ∈ {-25, -15, 0}, Δ_rate ∈ {0, +100bp, +200bp})
